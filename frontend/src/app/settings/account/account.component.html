What happens if we upgrade, downgrade, or cancel in the middle of a billing cycle?

If you upgrade or downgrade you’ll be charged the new rate for your new plan starting on your next billing cycle. If you cancel, you’ll be cancelled immediately and won’t be charged again.

Currently we accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We do not accept PayPal, checks, or POs. Sorry for any inconvenience.
